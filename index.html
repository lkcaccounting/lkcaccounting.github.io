Our Story

A Fresh Perspective on Small Business Finance

LKC Accounting was founded in 2025 with one mission: to make professional accounting services accessible to everyday Kenyan entrepreneurs.

As an accounting student, I bring current, practical knowledge directly to your business — without the overhead of a big firm. I understand the unique challenges faced by mama mbogas, barbers, butchery owners, and salonists.

We don't just crunch numbers. We give you insights that help you make better decisions, plug financial leaks, and grow your profits — explained in simple, plain language.
