About Us

Empowering Kenyan Small Businesses

Founded in 2025, LKC Accounting (Ledger & Knowledge Consulting) was born from a simple observation: small business owners in Kenya needed affordable, professional accounting services that understood their unique challenges.

From Mama Mbogas to barbers, butcheries to salons, we've helped over 100 small business owners transform their financial management. We believe every business deserves professional accounting, regardless of size.
