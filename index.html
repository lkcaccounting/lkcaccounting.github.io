Our Services

Comprehensive accounting solutions designed specifically for small business owners who need clarity and control over their finances.

Accounting Records Cleanup

Transform chaotic financial records into organized, actionable data. We specialize in fixing messy Excel files and restoring order to your accounts.

  • Fix messy Excel files
  • Reconcile cash, sales, and expenses
  • Restore missing or inconsistent records

Loss & Leakage Reviews

Uncover hidden financial drains in your business. Our thorough reviews identify where your money is going and why.

  • Identify unexplained losses
  • Check internal controls
  • Highlight weak areas in cash handling

Bookkeeping Support

Ongoing support to keep your books balanced and your business compliant. Perfect for busy entrepreneurs who need regular financial oversight.

  • Ongoing tracking of income & expenses
  • Simple monthly summaries
  • Owner-friendly reports
